About the Project

Hello! My name is Krystian and I'm the owner of WebDev, a boutique software company. I work as a full-stack developer and technical development lead in a large online printing company. I'm also a passionate runner and data enthusiast.

Together with my personal coach – a professional trainer and sports rehabilitation specialist – I’m developing a prototype application that integrates with the Garmin Connect API. The goal is to help me better understand my performance metrics, recovery trends, and training efficiency.

In the future, this tool may evolve into a solution to support other athletes coached by my trainer.
